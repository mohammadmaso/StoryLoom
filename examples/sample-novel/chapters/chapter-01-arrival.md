# Chapter 1: Arrival

[[Sarah]] stood before [[The Old Mansion]], clutching the [[Silver Key]] her uncle [[Marcus]] had mailed her the week before he vanished.

The estate loomed against a gray sky. She told herself she wasn't afraid of the dark—not anymore—but her hands trembled as she fit the key into the lock.

Inside, dust hung in the air like a veil. A portrait of Marcus watched from the stairwell, eyes following her every step.
